About the Doctor

Healing hearts with precision and empathy

Dr. Maaz Khan is a board-certified cardiologist at Apollo Heart Centre, Mumbai — a graduate of AIIMS New Delhi and fellowship-trained at Johns Hopkins. He combines world-class expertise with warmth that puts every patient at ease.

  • MBBS, MD – AIIMS New Delhi (Gold Medal)
  • Fellowship in Interventional Cardiology – Johns Hopkins
  • Member, Cardiological Society of India (CSI)
  • 50+ published papers in NEJM & Lancet
